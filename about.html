Corky's Associates

Are you straight out of coding bootcamp and can’t seem to get hired because of "lack of experience?" Well don't just sit there, do something about it!! Become a Corky Associate today!

There are some things you should know:

  • Corky’s Dev Shop LLC is a service to connect freelancers with a variety of skills to projects.
  • Corky’s is perfect for people straight out of coding bootcamp looking to gain experience and build their portfolio.
  • Associates provide their own quotes and invoices, Corky's just organizes it all.
  • Associates gain professional experience in their discipline of choice.
  • Associates have access to our slack channel, github, blog and benefit from one-on-one mentoring.
  • Associates are self-starters

Corky Associates are recognized as the best job candidates because they truly love what they do. There is no other way to be an associate. If this is your calling, visit the virtual front office for more information.

Corky's Process

Corky's Dev Shop can help:
