About Corky's Associates

Are you straight out of coding bootcamp and can’t seem to get hired because of ‘lack of experience.’ Well don't just sit there, do something about it!! Become a Corky Associate today.

There are some things you should know:

  • Corky’s Dev Shop LLC contracts with its associates to easily transfer employees onto new ventures.
  • Corky’s is perfect for people straight out of coding bootcamp looking to gain experience and build their portfolio.
  • Associates get paid on commission, meaning you’re responsible for finding projects that you want to work on, or helping other associates on their projects.
  • Associates gain professional experience in their discipline of choice. Associates have access to our slack channel, github and benefit from one-on-one mentoring.
  • Associates are self starters

You have to be willing to work hard for next to nothing to be a Corky Associate. Corky Associates are recognized as the best job candidates because they truly love what they do. There is no other way to be an associate. If this is your calling, email corky@corkysdevshop.com for more information.
